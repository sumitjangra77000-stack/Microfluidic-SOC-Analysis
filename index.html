Microfluidic Pressure Avalanches

A Study of Self-Organized Criticality (SOC) in Porous Networks

Project Overview

Ye project complexity science ke principles ko use karke microchannel networks mein "pressure avalanches" ko study karta hai. Humne dikhaya hai ki kaise chhote-chhote clogs milkar bade system failures trigger kar sakte hain.

Simulation Details

  • Platform: NetLogo
  • Concept: Bak-Tang-Wiesenfeld (BTW) Sandpile Model
  • Key Findings: Avalanche sizes follow a Power-Law distribution.
